About This Hack

With the current rise in school shooting every year, we want to have a system that protects people from these dangers

Detect

Detect Dangerous Individuals

Using Google Cloud vision API, detect individuals who are carrying firearms

Notify

Notify Everyone Quickly

Using Twilio API, notify authorities, employees, and students quickly through text messages and phone calls

Trust

Establish Reliability with BlockChain

Using BlockChains to ensure responses are reliable

What we want to do

Rather than reactive we want to be proactive. We want to prevent school shootings before they escalate.

Individual Awareness

Making sure everyone is aware of dangerous situations and authorities are notified

Panic Reduction

Individuals are more aware of the situation and can act accordingly knowing that help is coming

Easy To Use

Making sure people can quickly convey information quickly in a tense situation

Reliable Responses

Know that the right action is being taken based on accurate responses
